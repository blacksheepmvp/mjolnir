# Mjolnir v0.2.7

## What’s New
- Safety Net activity to prevent soft‑lock when a screen would otherwise be left with an empty activity stack.
- Protection indicators: persistent notification reports coverage; settings/onboarding show protection dots on top/bottom cards.
- Display change handling: display listener marks safety‑net pending without surfacing on wake.
- New gestures:
  - BOTH: Home — sends Home to both screens.
  - FOCUS: Home — sends Home to the selected screen.
  - BOTH: Auto — launches the assigned Top app on top + Bottom app on bottom.
  - FOCUS: Auto — launches the user-defined app on the active screen.
- Gesture presets are now supported (including custom presets).
- Start on boot (Advanced only): choose BOTH: Auto or BOTH: Home.
- Settings files moved to the app folder.
- Major refactor touching all aspects of UI/UX.

## Notes
- Debug tile is the only way to bring SafetyNet to the foreground; otherwise it stays at the bottom of each display stack.
